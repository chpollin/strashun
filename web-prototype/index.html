About the Strashun Library Digital Interface

The Strashun Library Digital Interface provides access to the historical lending records of the Strashun Library of Vilna, one of the most important Jewish libraries in pre-Holocaust Europe.

The Collection

This digital archive contains over 5,000 borrowing transactions spanning four distinct periods (1902, 1903-1904, 1934, and 1940), documenting the reading habits and intellectual life of the Jewish community in Vilna.

Using the Interface

  • Search for specific books or borrowers using the search bars
  • Apply filters to focus on specific time periods or demographics
  • Explore the network visualization to discover connections
  • Export data for your own research and analysis
  • Share specific views using the share button

Data Sources

The data has been carefully transcribed from original ledgers and enriched with metadata from the National Library of Israel (NLI) and YIVO Institute for Jewish Research.

Credits
